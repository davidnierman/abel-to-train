Training Offerings

Half hour or one hour sessions dedicated totally to you, your fitness and daily functional goals. We take in consideration any/all current pre-existing conditions and physical limitations while guaranteeing to get you moving, mobile and sweaty.
One hour sessions dedicated to couples or groups of two. Each workout is crafted and programmed with each individual goals’ in mind while providing a fun partner dynamic.
One hour sessions dedicated to groups of 3-6 people. These sessions are programmed to be equally part mobility, part strength.
This package starts with an in-depth one hour starter session going over all of your dietary habits – from how you stock your pantry and fridge to the way you order out at restaurants. The purpose of this program is to help teach you healthier consumption habits, provide you with the tools to make educated food choices and to inspire to get yourself in the kitchen a bit more! We go over recipes that fit your lifestyle and each program is entirely customized to you and your goals. Together, we track everything from your water consumption to meals and snacks.
Personal training sessions (in person or virtual) crafted around your individual health conditions and needs. As someone living with Crohn’s Disease, I understand that flare ups can affect our movement in daily life. These sessions are programmed with your health in mind, to minimize flares and still provide healthy movement when you are in the middle of having one. Sessions are heavily focused on mobility and flexibility work.
Personal training sessions targeted towards athletes, either coming off of an injury and/or those hyper focused of injury prevention. These sessions are highly focused on three- dimensional mobility work and personally tailored sports-focused exercises.

About

Aarin has been an athlete her entire life. From club soccer to competitive water polo, Aarin played rugby during her collegiate days at the University of Southern California. Exercise and fitness has always been an important part of Aarin’s lifestyle, but after being diagnosed with Crohn’s Disease in 2017, Aarin struggled with finding ways to maintain her fitness. Even doing functional tasks like carrying groceries up two flights of stairs proved to be challenging. Wanting to educate and help herself get back to an active and self-sufficient lifestyle, Aarin decided to pursue an education in personal fitness. In 2018 Aarin earned her personal training certification from the American Council of Exercise (ACE).

With her new fitness education Aarin trained herself from carrying groceries up the stairs to getting back to running a 10K races. She also started training clients in Los Angeles, California, including those with fellow auto-immune diseases, showing them that a self-sufficient and active lifestyle is possible.

Knowing that a healthy lifestyle is a holistic approach, Aarin decided to dive into nutritional studies. Aarin has earned her certification in Nutritional Coaching in 2020 from ACE and later on continued her education in functional movement and the benefits of cross training and preparedness by earning her CrossFit® Level 1 Trainer certification in 2022.

From 2021-2022 Aarin was also trained in Applied Function Science (AFS) during her time at Rehab United, an esteemed sports rehab and physical therapy clinic in San Diego, California. AFS emphasizes three dimensional functional movement and mobility which mirrors how we move in daily life. During her time at Rehab United, Aarin greatly enjoyed her time working with all populations but specifically older adults. Aarin finds its extremely rewarding strengthening those later in life to be able to achieve functional daily tasks, not much different from her love on training those living with auto-immune diseases.

When Aarin isn’t working out at her home gym you can catch her surfing, hiking, camping out of her rooftop tent (her dream is to hit every National Park in her lifetime) loving up on her lab- mix Jesse, cooking healthy meals and dancing the night away with her friends under the techno disco ball.
